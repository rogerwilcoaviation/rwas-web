# SEO Content Roadmap

Use this after the GSC export, not before. The site now has the foundational commercial pages, so the next content should target real impressions and near-page-one opportunities.

## Near-Term Page/Content Candidates

1. Garmin installer Sioux Falls / KFSD
   - Strengthen `/services/garmin-installation-northern-plains` or create a short support page only if GSC shows this query family.
2. GFC 500 eligibility and install planning
   - Add aircraft-eligibility examples, downtime expectations, and what owners should send for a quote.
3. ADS-B Out compliance in the Northern Plains
   - Good for owners searching compliance/transponder language rather than Garmin product names.
4. NDT use cases
   - Eddy current for cracks/corrosion, dye penetrant for surface defects, magnetic particle for ferrous parts.
5. Papa-Alpha support articles
   - PA-28 rigging, stabilator rigging, bell crank setup, and tool selection.
6. Pre-buy inspection buyer guide
   - Logbook red flags, avionics review, NDT triggers, and when to involve a repair station.

## Internal Linking Rules

- Blog posts should link to one relevant service page and one contact/quote path.
- Product pages for install-heavy Garmin equipment should link to the relevant service page.
- Collection pages should link to `/services` or the matching service page when the product category implies installation.

## Do Not Do

- Do not publish thin weekly posts just to publish.
- Do not chase national Garmin SKU rankings as the primary growth strategy.
- Do not write KFSD relocation copy that outpaces approved operational reality.
