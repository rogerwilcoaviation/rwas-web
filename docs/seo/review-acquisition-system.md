# Review Acquisition System

Goal: turn completed work into honest Google reviews without making the process awkward for customers or staff.

## Trigger

Send a review request after a completed job when:

- the customer has picked up the aircraft or received the shipped product,
- no unresolved billing or maintenance dispute is active,
- the team would be comfortable asking the customer directly.

## Email Template

Subject: Thanks from Roger Wilco Aviation Services

Hi {{first_name}},

Thanks again for trusting RWAS with {{aircraft_or_project}}. If the experience was useful, would you be willing to leave a short Google review?

{{google_review_link}}

A sentence or two about what we helped with is enough. Reviews help other aircraft owners find a shop for Garmin avionics, maintenance, NDT, fabrication, and pre-buy support in the Northern Plains.

Thank you,

Roger Wilco Aviation Services  
(605) 299-8178

## SMS Template

Thanks for working with RWAS on {{aircraft_or_project}}. If you have a minute, a Google review helps other aircraft owners find us: {{google_review_link}}

## Staff Rule

Ask for a review only once. Do not offer discounts, gifts, or incentives for reviews. Do not screen customers by asking only happy customers to review.

## Needed Before First Send

Add the final Google review URL from the Google Business Profile dashboard. The preferred format is the short review link Google provides inside GBP.

Once the link is confirmed, add it to the CRM/email signature snippets and use `?utm_source=review_request&utm_medium=email` only if Google preserves the URL safely.
