About Roger Wilco Aviation Services

A small-town repair station in Yankton, South Dakota — wheels-up since 2022, putting avionics, airframe, and powerplant work into general-aviation and light-turbine aircraft the old way: careful, certificated, and on the phone with you the whole time.

Roger Wilco Aviation Services opened its hangar doors at Chan Gurney Municipal Airport (KYKN) in 2022. We’re an FAA Part 145 Certificated Repair Station (Certificate No. RWSR491E), an authorized Garmin dealer, and members of both the Aircraft Electronics Association and the National Business Aviation Association.

We don’t pretend to be old. The airplanes are old — the shop is new, the tooling is current, and the standards are exactly what the FAA inspectors expect to see when they walk through the door. That’s the point.

Our bread and butter is avionics install and airframe / powerplant work on certified piston singles and twins, with a growing book of light turbine work. If you need an ADS-B install, a radio swap, a gear-rig tool, an annual, or a panel rebuild, that’s what this shop is built for.

Papa-Alpha Tools is our in-house line of rigging and service tooling — the stuff our mechanics wanted but couldn’t buy off a shelf, now available to other shops and owner-operators.

Jerry is our shop dog, unofficial FOD inspector, and the one member of the crew who never takes a weekend off.
